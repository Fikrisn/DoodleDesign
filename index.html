💡 Drawing Tips

🎨
Start Simple
Begin with basic shapes and outlines. The AI works best with clear, simple sketches that it can interpret and enhance.
📏
Use Clean Lines
Try to draw with confident, clean strokes. Avoid excessive sketchy lines as they can confuse the AI enhancement process.
🔍
Focus on Details
Add key details and features that you want the AI to recognize and enhance. The more context you provide, the better the results.
🎯
Practice Different Subjects
Try drawing various subjects - faces, objects, animals, landscapes. Each type of drawing can produce unique and surprising AI enhancements.
⚡
Experiment with Styles
The AI can adapt to different drawing styles. Try sketching in different approaches to see how the enhancement varies.
💭
Be Patient
AI processing takes a moment. While waiting, think about how you might improve your next sketch based on the enhanced results.
